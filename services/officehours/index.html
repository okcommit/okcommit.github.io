Office Hours

We provide one-off consultations to answer your OKR-related questions and help you diagnose adoption and process issues.

The hours can be used in various ways:

  • Answer OKR process questions or specific questions about your OKR implementation.
  • Diagnose “piping” issues with objectives and a lack of meaningful progress towards key results.
  • Discuss adoption challenges with the OKR process.
  • Answer specific questions about your implementation.
  • Provide quick assessments of your OKR implementation.

The office hours are appropriate for teams that are currently using OKRs.

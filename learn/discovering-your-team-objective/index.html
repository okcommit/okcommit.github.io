Discovering Your Team Objective

The following questions are helpful in discovering your team objective:

  • How can we contribute to one of the strategic objectives?

  • Which of the key results associated with a strategic objective can we impact?

  • Which area of our team’s performance would we want to improve the most to achieve a strategic objective?

  • What area of excellence for our team could we improve to an even higher level to ensure the organization achieves a strategic objective?

  • What area of poor performance for our team do we need to improve the most to ensure the organization achieves a strategic objective?
